DEVELOPING NEW PA-CANG VILLAGE, CA DY COMMUNE, NAM GIANG DISTRICT

Share via copy link

Link

  Time: 01/11/2023 - 01/11/2028 (5 years)

  Place: New Pa-Cang residential cluster, Pa-Ong village, Ca-Dy commune, Nam Giang district, Quang Nam province, Vietnam

2000

The new Pa Cang residential cluster is located on the beautiful Dakmy river, behind are the gentle Truong Son mountains. The village has 30 Co Tu households, of which 26 are poor (income less than 1,500,000 VND/household/month) and 4 near-poor households. Every day people go to the forest or to the river to look for food or items that can be sold. The biggest worry of the people here is lack of food and clothing, especially in the rainy season.

Recognizing the difficulties of the people, the charity company Smile Eye Charity proposed the project develop this residential cluster. The project calls for the support and participation of organizations and individuals to carry out practical training programs for people. The project will accompany people, support them and provide practical guidance for people to do and understand. From there, people improve their economic capacity, which is the root of sustainable development.

Bringing a "warm" Tet to the highland people of Nam Giang

Date posted: January 17, 2022

Author: Youth Union Editor

January 14-15, 2022, with the help of Nam Giang District Police, Provincial Police Youth Union coordinated with the People's Voice of Ho Chi Minh City, the Charity Association "Little Sister Bo and Friends" organized the "Border Spring" Program in 03 border communes of Cho Chun, La Eê and Dak Pring, Nam Giang district, Quang Nam province awarded 500 gifts worth more than 400 million VND for highland people.

These are communes bordering Se Kong province, Laos, including Cho Chun and La Eê are the two most remote communes of Nam Giang district with more than 90% of the population being ethnic minorities. Many areas do not have mobile phone signals, roads to commune centers and villages are very difficult. During the rainy season, you cannot travel by motorbike. Hunger and poverty still haunt the lives of our people here.

Each Tet gift includes 500,000 VND in cash and essential necessities such as warm clothes, rice, sugar, water. fish sauce, cooking oil, monosodium glutamate, milk, cakes, candy... not afraid of difficulties and obstacles, still overcoming mountains and forests to the poor people in the mountainous region of Nam Giang. Many people live on remote frontiers Unexpectedly receiving meaningful Tet gifts, I couldn't help but feel emotional.

On this occasion, the Volunteer Group visited, encouraged and gave Tet gifts to the Public Forces The official commune security is on duty to protect political security and ensure social order and safety in the country The 03 border communes mentioned above, each gift is worth 1 million VND.

Meaningful gifts before the New Year of the Tiger 2022 show the sincere feelings of the year Quang Nam police youth and sponsors share the hardships of people and contribute to bringing prosperity to a full, joyful and happy traditional Tet for the people in the border highlands.
